How Payouts Work

All bets contribute to a total prize pool. Payouts are calculated using a payout factor that ensures winning bets share the pool proportionally.

üèÜ Winning Bets

The payout factor is calculated as:

Payout Factor = (Total Prize Pool - Total Tied Bets) √∑ Total Won Bets

Your payout = Your Bet Amount √ó Payout Factor
