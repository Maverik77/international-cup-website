About International Cup

A celebration of golf, friendship, and fierce competition at Lansdowne

The International Cup is all about bringing people together for some friendly competition and a whole lot of fun. Every year, golfers from all over the world team up for two action-packed days where Team USA goes head-to-head with Team International. Think of it like the Presidents Cup—only with Lansdowne's own flavor and energy.

Founded by Phil Woodisse and Tim Pearce in 2021, the event has quickly grown into one of the club's most talked-about traditions. Now celebrating its 5th year, the International Cup isn't just a tournament—it's a weekend full of rivalries, laughs, and great memories both on and off the course.

Tournament Format

144 total points up for grabs across two days of championship golf

Points System

Each team battles for 72 points per day, with the first team to reach 73 points claiming the International Cup!

🤝

Day 1: Team Matches

Three team matches featuring three different formats—each match awards 2 points to the winner (1 point each for ties).

3 Matches × 2 Points 72 Points Available
⛳

Best Ball (Holes 1-6)

Partners each play their own ball, taking the better score on each hole. Pure shot-making with a safety net.

6 Holes Team Format
🏌️‍♂️

Alternate Shot (Holes 7-12)

Partners take turns hitting the same ball—ultimate teamwork, strategy, and pressure under fire.

6 Holes Team Format
🎯

Shamble (Holes 13-18)

Both players tee off, choose the best drive, then play their own ball from there. Risk-reward at its finest.

6 Holes Team Format
⚔️

Day 2: Singles Matches

Twenty-four individual battles with 3 points per match: front 9, back 9, and overall match winner.

24 Matches × 3 Points 72 Points Available
