About International Cup

A celebration of golf, friendship, and fierce competition at Lansdowne

The International Cup is all about bringing people together for some friendly competition and a whole lot of fun. Every year, golfers from all over the world team up for two action-packed days where Team USA goes head-to-head with Team International. Think of it like the Presidents Cup‚Äîonly with Lansdowne's own flavor and energy.

Founded by Jeff Miller, Scott Covino, Phil Woodisse and Tim Pearce in 2021, the event has quickly grown into one of the club's most talked-about traditions. Now celebrating its 5th year, the International Cup isn't just a tournament‚Äîit's a weekend full of rivalries, laughs, and great memories both on and off the course.
